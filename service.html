HR services
The HR services term goes back to the 20th century. Back then it was just about personnel administration and did not include employment relationships, policies and compliance, and other services at the organizational performance level. This service has evolved and now deals with a wide range of strategic functions that contribute directly to a company's success, such as talent management, employee development, organizational culture, and legal compliance.

Core HR Functions
  • Recruitment and Selection involves attracting, screening, and selecting qualified candidates to fill open positions within the organization.
  • The Employee Onboarding process ensures new employees have a smooth transition into the company culture and are equipped with the necessary tools and information to succeed.
  • Compensation and Benefits include determining employee salaries, wages, and benefits packages, ensuring fair and competitive compensation.
  • Performance Management involves setting performance expectations, providing feedback, and evaluating employee performance to drive productivity and achieve organizational goals.
  • Training and Developmententails providing employees with the necessary skills and knowledge to enhance their performance and career growth.
  • Employee Relations involves managing employee concerns, resolving conflicts, and fostering positive relationships between employees and the organization.

For businesses engaged in social media marketing, understanding GST compliance is crucial. Periodic GST returns must be filed, including GSTR-1, GSTR-3B, and GSTR-9.
