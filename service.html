HR services

The HR services term goes back to the 20th century. Back then it was just about personnel administration and did not include employment relationships, policies and compliance, and other services at the organizational performance level. This service has evolved and now deals with a wide range of strategic functions that contribute directly to a company's success, such as talent management, employee development, organizational culture, and legal compliance.
