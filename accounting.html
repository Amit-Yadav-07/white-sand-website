GST Registration Goods and Services Tax (GST) is a significant indirect tax imposed on the supply of goods and services within India. For businesses engaged in social media marketing, understanding GST compliance is crucial. GST Registration is a one-time process to enroll the business with the GST authorities.

ITR Filing is the process of submitting your Income Tax Return (ITR) to the Indian Income Tax Department. The ITR filing is a mandatory requirement for businesses earning above the taxable income threshold. This filing helps determine your tax liability, claim deductions and exemptions, and ensure compliance with tax laws. Our experts will assist you in preparing and filing your Income Tax Returns (ITR) accurately and on time.

GST Filing is an ongoing process that involves submitting periodic returns, claiming ITC, and ensuring accurate record-keeping. Periodic GST returns must be filed, including GSTR-1, GSTR-3B, and GSTR-9. Businesses can claim an input tax credit (ITC) on GST paid on inputs and input services. Accurate record-keeping is crucial for GST compliance. Failure to comply can lead to penalties and interest.
